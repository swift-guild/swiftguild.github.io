Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

This app is developed and operated by Swift Guild ("we", "our", or "the developer").

1. Data Storage

All food logs, macro tracking data, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device.

If iCloud Sync is enabled, data is stored securely in your private iCloud account. This data is not accessible to the developer.

2. Health Data

If you grant permission, the app may read from and/or write to Apple Health (HealthKit). Health data is used solely to provide app functionality and is never sold, shared for advertising, or transmitted to external servers.

3. Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information such as feature usage and app performance. This data does not include food logs or personal health data and is used solely to improve the app experience.

4. Food Database Searches

When searching for food items, the app sends search queries to third-party public food databases to retrieve nutritional information. These services may process technical data such as IP addresses as part of standard internet communication.

5. AI Food Scanning

If you use the AI food scanning feature, food images may be securely transmitted to an external AI processing service for analysis. Images are used solely to provide results and are not stored by the developer.

6. Data Sharing

Swift Guild does not sell, rent, or trade personal data.
